This plan targets individuals looking for sustained and stable income over a minimum of 12 months. Features include:

  • Annual returns ranging from 7% to 15%
  • Lower exposure to short-term market volatility
  • Backed by completed or income-generating commercial properties

5.2 Short-Term Investment

Designed for flexible and quicker returns, this option suits investors seeking liquidity and relatively lower risks:

  • Duration starts from one month
  • Monthly returns ranging from 6% to 15%
  • Withdrawals and reinvestments allowed at the end of each period

  • Investors own shares in property and share in resale profits
